Latitude vs. Wind Speed

We can see from this chart that the correlation between a cities latitude and wind speed is almost non-existant. We can come to this conclusion because we see such a wide range of wind speed levels at every latitudinal point. If any wind speed can occur at any longitudinal point we can conclude a weak correlation.

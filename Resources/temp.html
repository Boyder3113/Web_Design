Latitude vs. Max Temperature

This chart plots a cities location via latitude and the average temperature for each city. After plotting it looks like there is a correlation between citt latitude and temperature. We can see that the highest temperatures exist between -20 and 20 degrees latitude, 0 degrees latitude represents the equator so we expect to see the warm temps we are seeing in the latitude range close to 0 and we also expect to see temperatures dropping the further away we get from 0 degrees lat!
